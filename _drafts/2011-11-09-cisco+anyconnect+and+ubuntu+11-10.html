It's worked well for me from Ubuntu 10.04 to 11.04, but when I updated to 11.10, it broke.

What actually happens is that the AnyConnect client presents me with a dialogue to get my username and password. That works fine, but then I'm supposed to be presented with a challenge question. The challenge question doesn't appear. I only get the box to put the answer in. So I can't get logged in because there's no way to answer the challenge without knowing the question.

Googling didn't find an answer. But one thing I stumbled on is that it looks like the default Java in this version is OpenJava, not Oracle's. Hmmm.
